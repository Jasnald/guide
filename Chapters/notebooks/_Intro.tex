\part{Notebooks}
\label{part:notebooks}

The \texttt{notebook/} directory contains three Jupyter notebooks used for interactive inspection of intermediate and final results. They are \emph{read-only diagnostic tools}: they do not alter any pipeline data and can be executed independently of the main workflow.

\centering

\hline
\textbf{File} & \textbf{Experiment} & \textbf{Purpose} \\
\hline
\texttt{e1\_sim\_plot.ipynb}  & Exp1 & 3-D / 2-D surface reconstruction from polynomial JSON \\
\texttt{e2\_sim\_plot.ipynb}  & Exp2 & 1-D curve-fit quality check and overlay plot \\
\texttt{inp\_bc\_viewer.ipynb} & Both & Boundary-condition displacement map from \texttt{.inp} file \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
