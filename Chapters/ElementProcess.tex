\chapter{Element and Mesh Processing}
\label{ch:element_process}

\textbf{Module Path:} \texttt{src/ElementProcess/}

This module handles the mapping of stress fields onto Abaqus finite element meshes. Depending on the data source, the workflow splits into two distinct paths:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Analytical Workflow (3 Steps):} Generates synthetic stress fields for benchmarking.
    \item \textbf{Data-Driven Workflow (2 Steps):} Maps experimental/simulation data (from HDF5) directly onto the mesh.
\end{enumerate}
